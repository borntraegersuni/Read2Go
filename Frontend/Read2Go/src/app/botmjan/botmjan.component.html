Why We Chose This Book:

"Fourth Wing" introduces readers to a thrilling world of dragon riders, intense competition, and a protagonist who must defy the odds to survive. With rich world-building and a gripping narrative, this book has quickly become a favorite among fantasy readers.

About the Author:

Rebecca Yarros delivers another compelling story filled with depth, emotion, and exhilarating action. Her ability to craft intricate character relationships and high-stakes conflicts makes "Fourth Wing" an unforgettable read.

Book Description:

Violet Sorrengail was supposed to enter the Scribe Quadrant, living a quiet life among books. Instead, she is thrust into the dangerous world of dragon riders, where survival is anything but guaranteed.

With enemies at every turn and a war brewing beyond the walls of Basgiath War College, Violet must use every ounce of her intelligence and resilience to make it through.
